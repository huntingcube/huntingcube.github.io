Hunting

Player fights against a giant monster. Each attack of monster might be risky, So Player should dodge, or canceling his attack.

Slaying

Slaying monster gives the player some items. Items make player attack very well, And the option of the items keep being stacked. So that, Player gets stronger following the cubes.
